# Dedication

I dedicate this textbook to my father, [Thomas Zhang](https://ginsengpress.com/about-ginseng-press/).

In 2023, his wisdom guided me as my plans for graduate school were changed to
leading my sister's recovery program with her schizophrenia diagonosis. Not long
after, he showed an even deeper courage of his own, meeting a severe stroke with
strength, courage, and honor.
