# Dedication

I dedicate this textbook to my father, [Thomas Zhang](https://ginsengpress.com/about-ginseng-press/).

In 2023, his wisdom guided me as my plans to pursue a PhD were changed to leading
my sister's recovery program with her schizophrenia diagonosis. Not long after,
he showed an even deeper courage of his own, meeting a severe stroke with
strength, courage, and honor.
